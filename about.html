About Me

Passion

I have a passion for designing clean, efficient websites that are responisive and tech forward. I love to learn and I learn very quickly. I strive for excellence in everything I do but I also know when to ask for help, and in that way I remain humble. There is always something new to learn when it comes to programming which makes it a perfect field for me because I know I will never get tired of learning new things.

Personal

Arya, our cat, is the sweetest cat you will ever meet and Ollie, our puppy, is surprisingly well-behaved for being only 4 months old. I love coffee, travelling to new places, and coloring. But the most important thing to me is my relationship with Jesus and getting to serve him everyday as part of the church and in everything I do.
